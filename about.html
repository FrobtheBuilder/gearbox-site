About Us

Above you can see the Grady Gearbox Gangstaz (Grady High School's robotics team) in action. Our team constructs robots that compete in the BEST and FIRST competitions. Additionally, we are involved in our community promoting science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) in various ways. Our robotics team was started in 2004, going through various stages of community involvement that has lead us to our largest year yet.
